## 2. Problem Statement
- SMB digital advertising is fragmented across multiple platform dashboards (Google, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok), demanding significant manual tuning.
- Delayed optimization analysis causes 50-70% of ad spend budget to be wasted.

---

## 3. Technical Architecture & Google Cloud Integration
- **Features**:
  - Full Ad Lifecycle automation: brief ingestion, asset creation, and campaign deployment.
  - Multi-agent mesh: orchestrates 60+ autonomous **Gemini 2.0 Flash** agents running on Google Cloud Run.
  - Active Optimization: real-time performance adjustments, budget reallocation across networks, and AEO/GEO semantic channel audits.
- **Platform**: Powered by the modern GoogleGenAI SDK.

---

## 4. Business Case & ROI
- Consolidates Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, and TikTok campaign management.
- Cuts down ad budget waste by reacting instantly to performance signals.
- Standardizes cross-network tracking telemetry.
