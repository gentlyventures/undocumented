# Competitor Profile: LedgerAudit

## 1. Project Information
- **Testing Access**: Simulated inside UnDocumented platform.
- **Problem Space**: Search citation audits, brand visibility mapping, and SEO optimization.

---

## 2. Problem Statement
- Companies that rely on traditional search engine optimization (SEO) are not optimized for LLM-based search and generative query systems.
- Detecting citation gaps, mapping brand references, and verifying output citation quality across diverse model endpoints is highly manual.

---

## 3. Technical Architecture & Google Cloud Integration
- **Features**:
  - Web Crawler: fetches page content from targeted domains to index text assets.
  - Multi-LLM Citation Auditing: integrates Groq (Llama 3) and Gemini 1.5 Flash models to identify citation gaps and suggest content edits.
  - Reporting Dashboard: logs citation frequencies, missing brand mentions, and optimization advice.

---

## 4. Business Case & ROI
- Enhances brand presence and search accuracy in generative AI outputs.
- Automates content adjustments to align with search patterns.
