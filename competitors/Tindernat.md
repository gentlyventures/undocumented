## 2. Problem Statement
- Universities in Central and Eastern Europe assign student roommates manually using static spreadsheets.
- The lack of compatibility checks (sleep cycles, cleanliness, study habits) causes student stress, conflicts, and administrative overhead.

---

## 3. Technical Architecture & Google Cloud Integration
- **Features**:
  - Structured Questionnaire: collects lifestyle data from students.
  - Matching Agent: runs a weighted compatibility pairing algorithm built on **Google Agent Development Kit (ADK)**.
  - Admin Dashboard: housing administrators review, override, and publish room assignments.
  - Student Information Systems integration: connects to university database environments via **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**.
  - Explainable pairings: surfaces AI rationale behind recommended roommate matches.

---

## 4. Business Case & ROI
- Automates administrative manual spreadsheet sorting.
- Reduces university roommate conflict incident rates and room-change logistics requests.
- Elevates student satisfaction and academic performance.
