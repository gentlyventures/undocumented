## 2. Problem Statement
- B2B sellers cannot identify active evaluators vs. passive browsers. Inbound forms, cold outreach, and third-party intent data are slow, anonymous, or account-level.

---

## 3. Technical Architecture & Google Cloud Integration
- **Platform**: Personal digital business card and buyer intent portal.
- **Features**:
  - Personal profile for sellers shared via email signatures, DMs, or QR codes.
  - LLM Assistant on the profile grounded in product docs to answer buyer questions.
  - Topic modeling over questions to map buyer-intent themes (pricing, integration, security, competitive, implementation, ROI).
  - Scores conversations (Hot/Warm/Cold) and syncs contact details and intent data directly to the seller's CRM.
- **Ecosystem Integration**: CRM sync, document grounding.
