# Competitor Profile: ComplianceGuard

## 1. Project Information
- **Testing Access**: Simulated inside UnDocumented platform.
- **Code Repository**: [compliance-guard-sim](https://github.com/compliance-guard/compliance-guard.git)

---

## 2. Problem Statement
- Employee management in SMEs is reactive, resulting in early turnover because traditional metrics fail to generate predictive Training & Development (T&D) or People & Culture (P&C) plans.

---

## 3. Technical Architecture & Google Cloud Integration
- **Features**:
  - Predicts early turnover risks by analyzing meeting frequency and team feedback.
  - Automatic Function Calling: uses **Gemini 1.5 Flash** to cross-reference data and propose mitigating T&D learning paths.
  - Workspace Integrations: queries calendars and document metadata as engagement indicators.
  - Analytics: cleans and formats HR telemetry using standard data science packages.
  - Database: uses a local database to track employee DISC profiles and risk vectors.

---

## 4. Business Case & ROI
- Lowers recruiting and early-turnover overhead by proactively identifying disengagement signals.
- Automates the alignment of performance drops with direct mitigation actions.
