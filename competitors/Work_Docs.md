# Competitor Profile: Work Docs

## 1. Project Information
- **Testing Access**: [theworkdocs.com](https://www.theworkdocs.com/)
- **Demo Video**: [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UdkN_F9yCdc&t=3s)

---

## 2. Problem Statement
- Self-learners struggle to extract and retain knowledge from dense PDFs, textbooks, and research papers because passive reading lacks active feedback.

---

## 3. Technical Architecture & Google Cloud Integration
- **Features**:
  - Semantic Chat: chat directly with uploaded documents to clarify concepts.
  - Automatic Structuring: auto-generates structured summaries and notes.
  - Mind Maps: visualizes structural and concept connections in a canvas layout.
  - Context-Aware Explanations: prompts users with personalized follow-up learning questions.

---

## 4. Business Case & ROI
- Transforms passive text consumption into active learning.
- Enhances long-term knowledge retention.
- Serves as a 24/7 personal tutor/study partner.
