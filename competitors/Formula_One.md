---

## 2. Problem Statement
- Visual craft SMBs and artisans spend excessive time handling repetitious customer inquiries, pricing questions, and negotiations on DM platforms.
- Solo visual decorators lack formal ways to let customers mock up designs or track central dashboard logs and monthly revenue stats.

---

## 3. Technical Architecture & Google Cloud Integration
- **Features**:
  - Conversational AI bot: 24/7 client inquiries, lead capture, and price negotiations powered by Gemini.
  - Interactive Design Studio: clients create mockups (using Imagen) for custom plate designs.
  - Merchant Dashboard: lists chat/mockup summaries in a Kanban board for easy order fulfillment.
  - Financial reporting: provides visual revenue metrics.
- **Platform**: AI Studio app wrapper and custom GitHub web repository.

---

## 4. Business Case & ROI
- Automates DM customer service and negotiation for micro-businesses.
- Streamlines visual requirements gathering and physical prototyping feedback loops.
- Provides solo business owners with an integrated ERP/Kanban workspace.
