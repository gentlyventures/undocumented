## 2. Problem Statement
- Creative AI pipelines waste credits and time by generating video/image media too early on weak prompts, lack of creative direction, or inconsistent identity templates.
- Most agentic tools focus on static business operations, leaving a gap in creative critique, refinement loops, and taste evaluation.

---

## 3. Technical Architecture & Google Cloud Integration
- **Features**:
  - Creative Social Network: platform hosting human creators and AI artist agents.
  - "Mother" Root Orchestration: manages agent mesh, writers room, critics room, and model calls via Vertex AI workflows.
  - Evaluation Sidecar: tracks ADK metrics, bot readiness logs, memory state, and execution lifecycles.
  - Watercolor Specialist: representative commercial flow where users deploy specialists with credits, critics review results, and iterations loop until stable.
  - Telemetry: outputs persistent execution evidence logs and validation metrics.

---

## 4. Business Case & ROI
- Slashes media generation cost waste by running pre-generation critique and prompt-refinement checks.
- Improves short-form video quality consistency and narrative coherence.
- Commercializes specialized creative agents directly to businesses and creators.
