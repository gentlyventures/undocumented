## 2. Problem Statement
- **Administrative Burden**: Dentists, clinicians, doctors, and medical administrative staff spend 60% or more of their time doing computer work rather than patient care.

---

## 3. Our Solution
- ARAGS automates clinical administrative computer workflows, giving time back to clinicians and administrative staff so they can focus on patient care.
